Ragdoll Kittens for sale - About Our Cattery

We are a modest Ragdoll breeder in Chapel Hill, NC. We are a TICA recognized Ragdoll cattery that has been breeding for many years and offers nothing but the best. We aim to breed healthy Ragdoll kittens with sweet, sensitive, and loving personalities. Our kittens are handled on a daily basis and are completely spoiled. All of our Ragdoll kittens and cats for sale are reared inside as part of our family, not in cages.

When it comes to the health of our cats and kittens, Ragdoll kittens Cattery makes no compromises. All of our adults and breeding Ragdolls have tested negative for HCM using DNA. They have all been blood typed and tested negative for FIV (also known as feline HIV or AIDS), FeLV (commonly known as feline leukemia virus), and feline heartworm disease using the SNAP feline triple combination test. We are a closed cattery, which means we do not provide stud services and do not allow cats other than our breeding family to access our house at any time. This protects our cats and kittens from feline diseases and maintains tight health control over our breeding environment. Our Ragdoll cats for sale are strictly indoors, fed high-quality food, and get a lot of human attention

Before leaving my home, all kittens will have been changed, de-wormed, vaccinated with age-appropriate immunizations (against Panleukopenia, Feline Rhinotracheitis, and Calicivirus), and will have the option of being microchipped. All kittens are guaranteed to be healthy and come with lifetime support if necessary.

If you're seeking for a truly amazing cat or kitten with a fantastic temperament, personality, and appearance, you've come to the right place.
